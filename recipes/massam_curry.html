AI Massam Curry

I love my massaman curry for two reasons: there's always enough for two and it makes me feel like I'm in a Thai restaurant. #ThaiFood

Description

Massam curry is a rich and flavorful Thai curry that is typically made with beef or lamb, but can also be made with chicken or tofu. Here is a recipe for Massam curry that serves 4-6 people:

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef or lamb, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 tablespoons Massam curry paste
  • 2 tablespoons palm sugar
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 large potato, peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 large carrot, peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

Steps

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat 1 cup of the coconut milk over medium heat. Add the Massam curry paste and cook, stirring constantly, until the paste is fragrant and well combined with the coconut milk.
  2. Add the beef or lamb to the pot and stir to coat the meat with the curry paste. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the meat is browned on all sides.
  3. Add the remaining 1 cup of coconut milk, water, cinnamon stick, bay leaves, palm sugar, tamarind paste, and fish sauce to the pot. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 1 hour or until the meat is tender.
  4. Add the potato, carrot, peas, and cherry tomatoes to the pot and continue to simmer for an additional 15-20 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Remove the cinnamon stick and bay leaves from the pot and discard. Serve the Massam curry hot, with rice or noodles. Enjoy!
